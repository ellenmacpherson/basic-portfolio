Who I am

  • A native Sydneysider, currently based in the UK.
  • I started out with degrees in history and politics and a professional background in customer service and content creation. I'm living proof that you don't need a computer science degree to be a developer.
  • In short, I'm a career changer with an interest in how tech affects the lives of people. I also really like designing websites.

What I can do

  • I work primarily with HTML, CSS (incl. Sass), and JavaScript and am also familiar with Python for web development and data analytics.
  • Full time, I help maintain and add new features to BT's TV platform, a service that goes out to millions of users every month. Most of my day job involves asking "but why?"
  • User-focused products are the best products. If you're trying to make something cool that's also something your mum could use, give me a shout. I'd be happy to have a chat about it.

The bigger picture

  • I am passionate about seeing more diversity in tech. I make up one of the 4% of software developers in the UK who identify as female, and I'm keen to get involved in making that a much larger number through making workplaces more inclusive.
  • I think coding is for everyone who wants to get involved. I firmly believe we cannot do a good job of hearing and representing the diversity of our customers views if we aren't diverse ourselves.
